About Us

Introducing Adopt a Trolley, Adopt a River's newest initiative paving the way to keeping parks, streets, curbs and social areas clean!

We have spent the past 2 years working towards making our rivers and beaches cleaner. However this entire time we haven't been able to put any effort towards cleaning up the litter that piles up everywhere else, but those times are over! Adopt a Trolley is taking a stand and making the effort to be the difference that we so desperately need.

We are collaborating with local businesses and the eThekweni Municipality to take back our streets and you can join the fight! Learn more about the half and full advertising packages that we offer!

We need your help!

Join the fight to create a brighter future for our communities and environment through donating or becoming our newest sponsor. Any and every contribution means the world to us!
